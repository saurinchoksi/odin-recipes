Luigi's Fire Flower Lasagna

Description

Hey, it's-a me, Luigi! I'mma tell you about lasagna, a tasty dish straight outta the Mushroom Kingdom! It's got layers of pasta, sauce, and cheese that'll make ya say "Mamma mia!" It's as comforting as finding a Super Star power-up when you're low on health. Trust me, I know my lasagna - I've eaten it all over the world, from Bowser's Castle to Yoshi's Island. So, next time you're lookin' for a meal that'll make ya feel like you've won the Star Cup, give lasagna a try! Wahoo! Luigi out!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground Koopa Troopa meat
  • 1 Super Mushroom, diced
  • Fire Flower petals, minced
  • 1 (28-ounce) can Hammer Bros. crushed tomatoes
  • 2 (6-ounce) cans Piranha Plant tomato paste
  • 1 tablespoon Bowser's Castle Italian seasoning
  • Salt and Poison Mushroom powder, to taste
  • 1 (16-ounce) package Warp Pipe lasagna noodles
  • 15 ounces Yoshi egg ricotta cheese
  • 1 large Birdo egg
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh Super Leaves
  • 1/2 cup grated Gold Coin Parmesan cheese
  • 4 cups shredded Super Star mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Brown ground Koopa Troopa meat, add diced Super Mushroom and minced Fire Flower petals. Sauté until tender.
  3. Add crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 30 minutes.
  4. Cook lasagna noodles until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  5. Mix ricotta cheese, egg, chopped parsley, and grated Parmesan.
  6. Layer sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, and shredded mozzarella in a baking dish.
  7. Bake for 25-30 minutes until golden and bubbly.
  8. Let cool briefly, slice, and serve hot. Enjoy your Mushroom Kingdom-inspired lasagna!
